YWCA Richmond

The goal of this project was a simple theme refresh. The client wanted their wordpress site to look cleaner, and more modern. To accomplish this task, I started with the "EcoNature" theme as a foundation. From there I made changes to how the content was displayed, including drastic changes to the header and footer.

Most of the pages on the client's site also had to be completely rewritten, as much of the text was free floating and dependant on inline styling.

This was a solo project completed in approximately 1 month.
