Simple Tips

  • Wash your hands after handling
  • Move slowly and don't squeeze them hard
  • Follow a routine with feeding and cleaning
  • Be careful during shedding, help them only if they need it
  • Keep the habitat warm, but have cool spots for your snake to hide
  • Avoid heat rocks as they damage the snake's sensitive skin
  • Do research to ensure you're feeding your snake properly
